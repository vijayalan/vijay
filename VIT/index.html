Welcome to Venkatesh Institute of Technology, where innovation meets excellence in the heart of Karambakudi, Tamil Nadu. Since our inception on January 25, 2023, we have been dedicated to shaping the future of healthcare education. Situated amidst the serene surroundings of Tamil Nadu, our institution provides a conducive environment for learning and growth.

At Venkatesh Institute of Technology, we offer a diverse range of courses tailored to meet the demands of the ever-evolving healthcare industry. From Diploma in Nursing to Diploma in Medical Lab Technician and Diploma in Operation Theater & Anesthesia, our programs are designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in their chosen fields. Additionally, we provide complimentary courses in interview skills, employability skills, placement skills, spoken skills, computer skills, entrepreneurship skills, and drawing & writing skills, ensuring holistic development of our students.

With a steadfast commitment to our students' success, we offer 100% placement assistance, guiding them towards promising careers in healthcare. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty further enhance the learning experience, fostering innovation and leadership in our students. Welcome to Venkatesh Institute of Technology, where dreams take flight and excellence knows no bounds.
